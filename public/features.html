Our Features

Powerful tools to help you land more interviews

Keyword Analysis

Our tool analyzes job descriptions to identify key skills, qualifications, and terminology that recruiters are looking for. We then scan your resume to see if these keywords are present and suggest additions where needed.

  • Identify missing keywords
  • Match industry-specific terminology
  • Improve keyword density

ATS Optimization

Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) can reject resumes before a human ever sees them. We ensure your resume is formatted correctly for these systems, with proper headings, fonts, and layouts that won't get filtered out.

  • ATS-friendly formatting
  • Proper section headings
  • Optimized file format

Content Improvement

Beyond keywords, we provide suggestions to strengthen your achievements, use more impactful language, and highlight relevant experience that aligns with the job description.

  • Achievement-focused language
  • Quantifiable results
  • Relevant experience highlighting

Format Suggestions

We analyze your resume's structure and provide recommendations to improve readability, organization, and visual appeal, ensuring recruiters can quickly find the information they need.

  • Improved readability
  • Better information hierarchy
  • Professional formatting
