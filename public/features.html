Our Features

ATS Optimization

Our system analyzes your resume against industry-standard Applicant Tracking Systems to ensure it gets past automated filters and into the hands of hiring managers.

Keyword Analysis

We extract important keywords from job descriptions and compare them with your resume, suggesting additions and modifications to increase your match rate.

Format Suggestions

Get recommendations on how to structure your resume for maximum readability and impact, including section ordering, spacing, and layout.

Content Improvement

Our AI analyzes your resume content and suggests improvements to make your achievements more impactful and your skills more relevant to the job you're applying for.

Resume Templates

Choose from a variety of professional templates designed to be both visually appealing and ATS-friendly.

Job Tracking

Keep track of your job applications, interviews, and follow-ups in one convenient dashboard.
