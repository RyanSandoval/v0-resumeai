Our Features

Keyword Analysis

Our AI scans job descriptions to identify key skills, qualifications, and terminology that recruiters are looking for. We then analyze your resume to see if these keywords are present and suggest additions where needed.

ATS Optimization

Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) can reject resumes before a human ever sees them. We ensure your resume is formatted correctly for these systems, with proper headings, fonts, and layouts that won't get filtered out.

Content Improvement

Beyond keywords, we provide suggestions to strengthen your achievements, use more impactful language, and highlight relevant experience that aligns with the job description.

Format Suggestions

We analyze your resume's structure and provide recommendations to improve readability, organization, and visual appeal, ensuring recruiters can quickly find the information they need.
